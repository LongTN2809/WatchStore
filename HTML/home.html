Our Story

Born from a passion for exceptional timepieces and a vision to make luxury accessible.

Our Beginning

Royal Time was founded in 2025 by Tang Nhat Long, a lifelong watch enthusiast who noticed a gap in the market. While luxury timepieces were often exclusive and intimidating, Long envisioned a place where anyone could discover, learn about, and acquire exceptional watches with confidence.


Starting with a small collection of carefully curated pieces, we've grown into a trusted destination for watch lovers worldwide, never losing sight of our core mission: making the art of horology accessible to all.

Our Philosophy

We believe that a great watch is more than just a timekeeper—it's a companion for life's most important moments. Every piece in our collection is selected not just for its technical excellence, but for its ability to tell a story and create lasting memories.


Our commitment extends beyond the sale. We provide comprehensive education, expert guidance, and lifetime support to ensure that every customer finds not just a watch, but their perfect timepiece.

Our Mission

Dedicated to bringing you exceptional timepieces while revolutionizing the luxury watch experience

  • Provide high-quality products at the best prices

    We source directly from manufacturers to offer premium timepieces at competitive prices without compromising on quality.

  • Prioritize customer experience above all

    Every interaction is designed to exceed expectations, from browsing to after-sales support.

  • Continuously update new models and trends

    Stay ahead of horological innovations and bring the latest designs to our discerning customers.

Driving Innovation in Horology

We believe that luxury should be accessible, authentic, and accompanied by exceptional service. Our mission extends beyond selling timepieces—we're building a community of watch enthusiasts who appreciate the artistry, heritage, and precision that defines exceptional horology.
